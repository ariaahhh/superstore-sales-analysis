# Superstore Sales — Findings Summary 📊

## Executive Summary
Analysis of 9,994 retail orders from a US Superstore (2014–2017) revealed 
$2.29M in total revenue and $286K in profit. While the business is growing 
year over year, 19% of orders are loss making — a critical area requiring 
immediate attention.

---

## 1. Revenue & Profit Overview

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Revenue | $2,297,200 |
| Total Profit | $286,397 |
| Total Orders | 9,994 |
| Unique Customers | 793 |
| Total Units Sold | 37,873 |

**Profit margin overall: 12.5%**

---

## 2. Category Performance

| Category | Revenue | Profit |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Technology | $836,154 | $145,454 |
| Furniture | $741,999 | $18,451 |
| Office Supplies | $719,047 | $122,490 |

⚠️ **Red flag — Furniture** generates high revenue but extremely low profit. 
Deep discounting or high costs are likely the cause. Needs investigation.

---

## 3. Product Insights

**Top performing products:**
- Canon imageCLASS 2200 Copier — $25,199 profit
- Fellowes PB500 Binding Machine — $7,753 profit
- Hewlett Packard LaserJet Copier — $6,983 profit

**Loss making products:**
- Cubify CubeX 3D Printer (Double) — -$8,879 loss
- Lexmark Laser Printer — -$4,589 loss
- Cubify CubeX 3D Printer (Triple) — -$3,839 loss

💡 **Recommendation:** Consider discontinuing 3D Printer line entirely.
Copiers and printing equipment are clear profit drivers — increase stock and promotion.

---

## 4. Regional Performance

| Region | Revenue | Profit |
|--------|---------|--------|
| West | Highest | Highest |
| East | Second | Second |
| Central | Third | Third |
| South | Lowest | Lowest |

💡 **Recommendation:** Investigate what West region is doing differently 
and replicate those strategies in South region.

---

## 5. Time Trends

- **Peak sales month:** November (Black Friday effect)
- **Revenue growing** year over year from 2014 to 2017
- **All 3 categories** show consistent growth

💡 **Recommendation:** Increase inventory and marketing spend in 
October–November to capitalise on peak season demand.

---

## 6. Customer Insights

- **793 unique customers** placing 9,994 orders — strong repeat buying (avg 12.5 orders per customer)
- **Top customer:** Sean Miller at $25,043 total spend
- **Consumer segment** dominates — more revenue and profit than Corporate and Home Office combined

💡 **Recommendation:** Launch a VIP loyalty programme targeting top 50 customers. 
Focus marketing budget on Consumer segment.

---

## 7. Profitability Analysis

| Category | Orders | Percentage |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Profitable | 8,058 | 80.6% |
| Loss Making | 1,871 | 18.7% |
| Break Even | 65 | 0.7% |

⚠️ **19% of orders are loss making** — largely driven by heavy discounting 
in Furniture and certain Technology products.

---

## 8. Profit Margin by Sub-Category

**Highest margins:**
- Copiers — highest profit margin
- Labels — second highest
- Envelopes — third highest

**Negative margins (losing money):**
- Tables — negative margin
- Bookcases — near zero margin

💡 **Recommendation:** Reduce or eliminate discounts on Tables and Bookcases. 
Review pricing strategy for Furniture sub-categories entirely.

---

## Conclusion

The Superstore is a growing business with strong Technology and Office Supplies 
performance. The key areas to address are:

1. **Furniture profitability** — high revenue, very low profit
2. **Loss making orders** — 19% of orders need discount strategy review  
3. **3D Printer product line** — consistently loss making, consider discontinuing
4. **South region** — underperforming vs other regions, needs strategy review
5. **Peak season preparation** — November is critical, plan inventory accordingly
